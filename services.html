MDR/XDR Solutions

CSA backs your organization with an intelligent and dedicated security team that works nonstop to protect your environment. Gain access to leading technical solutions that we can put into place or let us work seamlessly with any next-generation anti-virus and firewall threat tool you prefer. We manage MDR including full-time threat hunting along with alert monitoring, prioritization, investigation, analysis, and response—24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We apply artificial intelligence models to server, endpoint, and network data, correlating and investigating indicators to determine scope and probable impact. We then report back to you with a root cause analysis and targeted remediation recommendations.Our MDR solution at the endpoints begins with CrowdStrike Falcon, the leading breach prevention software in the world. We enhance your program even more by including SIEM capabilities that allow our team to correlate log events from your existing investments. Our security engineers and analysts work with you to create custom blocking procedures on devices you already own. Our flexible MDR capabilities are unique in the industry, and give you a truly customized solution that meets your specific requirements.

Zero-Trust Access

CSA offers a comprehensive zero-trust access solution with full security inspection (web filtering, AV, IPS, malware protection, and CASB). It includes Universal ZTNA, identity, multi-factor authentication, continuous posture assessment, adaptive and granular application access, and network access control (NAC). Our Universal ZTNA goes above and beyond industry-standard cloud-only ZTNA for remote access. Our offering provides flexible secure access to corporate and SaaS applications from all locations where a user might be working. To address the complexity, cost, and security gaps caused when deploying multiple endpoint solutions, CSA offers a single, integrated unified agent that combines secure access with ZTNA, endpoint protection (EPP), and endpoint detection and response (EDR). With the unified FortiClient endpoint agent, you can move from VPN to ZTNA and on-board applications at your own pace. In addition, integrated IT hygiene, endpoint protection, and secure application access capabilities, including vulnerability scanning, AV, web filtering, EDR, and sandbox integration, are included. CSA zero-trust controls integrate with the Fortinet Security Fabric to deliver automated and simplified operations. CSA acts as Fabric Agent to provide telemetry and endpoint visibility, along with posture with the FortiClient Endpoint Management Server (EMS). Our EMS connects with the Fabric to communicate near real-time endpoint information to all Fortinet zero-trust enforcement points that are part of the Fabric.
